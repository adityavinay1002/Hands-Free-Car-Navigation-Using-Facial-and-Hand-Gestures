GESTURE CONTROL TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE (Full AI Version)
=========================================================

STEP 1: CHECK DETECTION
-----------------------
Run `python main.py`.

1. **FACE TRACKING**: Do you see a mesh over your face?
   - **Move Head LEFT**: Does it say "STEERING: LEFT"?
   - **Move Head RIGHT**: Does it say "STEERING: RIGHT"?
   - **Note**: You must move your head physically left/right in the camera frame, not just rotate.

2. **HAND TRACKING**: Do you see skeleton on your hand?
   - **OPEN PALM**: "ACCELERATING (W)"
   - **FIST**: "BRAKING (SPACE)"
   - **VICTORY**: "REVERSING (S)"

STEP 2: GAMEPLAY ISSUES
-----------------------
1. **Game Ignored Keys**:
   - **CLICK THE GAME**. The browser MUST be the active window.
   - **Run as Administrator**.
   
2. **Car Not Acting Right**:
   - **Steering Weak?**: Move your head further to the side.
   - **Brake not working?**: Check if the game uses 'SPACE' for brake. If not, edit `main.py` to use expected key (e.g. 'down' arrow).
   
3. **Lag**:
   - Close other apps. MediaPipe uses CPU.

STEP 3: CRASHES
---------------
- Ensure `mediapipe==0.10.9` is installed.
